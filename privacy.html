Privacy Policy

Where Things Belong Ltd
Last updated: 30 March 2026

Who We Are

Where Things Belong Ltd provides professional decluttering and organising services in the UK.

If you have any questions about this policy or your data, contact:
Email: enquiries@wherethingsbelong.co.uk

What We Collect

We only collect information you choose to provide, including through our contact form, client intake form, or by email, such as:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number (if given)
  • Address (where relevant)
  • Details you share about your enquiry

Where relevant to the services, we may also create internal project records, including notes and photographs of rooms, layouts, storage areas, and belongings, for purposes such as assessment reference, proposal preparation, service planning, progress tracking, and internal administration.

We do not use analytics, advertising, or tracking tools.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Respond to enquiries
  • Arrange discovery calls or assessments
  • Prepare proposals and provide services
  • Maintain necessary business and client records
  • Plan, deliver, and review organising services, including through internal project notes and photographs where relevant

We do not sell your data or use it for marketing unless you have separately agreed to this.

Legal Basis

We process your information in order to:

  • Take steps at your request before entering into a contract
  • Perform our contract with you where services are agreed
  • Manage and operate our business, maintain records, and document service delivery where this is necessary for our legitimate interests and does not override your rights and interests
  • Where we wish to use photographs or other materials for marketing, portfolio, social media, testimonial, or educational purposes, we will only do so where you have given separate consent, where required.

Internal Project Photographs

Where relevant to the services, we may take photographs of rooms, layouts, storage areas, and belongings for internal purposes such as assessment reference, proposal preparation, service planning, progress tracking, project records, and internal administration. These images are used for internal service delivery purposes only unless you separately agree in writing to marketing, portfolio, testimonial, social media, or educational use.

How Long We Keep Information

  • Enquiry data (where no work proceeds): up to 12 months
  • Client records: up to 6 years for legal, tax, accounting, and business record-keeping purposes
  • Internal project records, including service-delivery notes and photographs where taken, are retained as part of the relevant client record unless a shorter retention period applies.

Hosting, Forms, Storage and Technical Data

Our website is delivered via Cloudflare for hosting and security. Cloudflare may process technical data such as IP addresses and access logs in order to provide these services.

We also use Google services, including Google Forms, Google Drive, and related Google Workspace tools, to collect, store, organise, and manage enquiry and client information as part of running the business.

These providers process personal data on our behalf in order to support website hosting and security, form collection, file storage, and day-to-day business administration.

We do not use cookies for analytics or advertising.

Your Rights

Under UK data protection law, you have the right to:

  • Request access to your personal data
  • Request correction or deletion, where applicable
  • Object to processing in certain circumstances
  • Request restriction of processing, where applicable
  • Request data portability, where applicable

You may also complain to the Information Commissioner’s Office (ICO) if you believe your data has been handled improperly.
