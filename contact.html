Get In Touch

Make an enquiry

You’re very welcome to get in touch. You do not need to know exactly what support you need at this stage. A brief overview is enough to get started, and from there we can work out whether the service is the right fit.

Fill out the contact form or email enquiries@wherethingsbelong.co.uk.

What happens next

Once I receive your message, I’ll reply with the next step. This will usually be a short intake form to gather a little more detail about your space and the kind of support you’re looking for.

From there, we can arrange a quick call if needed and work out what would be most helpful, including the most appropriate assessment.

There is no obligation at this stage. The aim is simply to understand what support would be most useful. All enquiries are handled with care and without judgement. If I’m not the right person to help, I’ll let you know honestly once I have enough information to do so.
