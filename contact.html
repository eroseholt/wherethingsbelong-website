Contact

If you’re considering working with me, you’re very welcome to get in touch. You do not need to know exactly what support you need at this stage. We can talk that through together and work out whether the service is the right fit. Reaching out is simply a starting point.

How to get in touch

Fill out your contact details below and include a short message, or email enquiries@wherethingsbelong.co.uk.

Both come directly to me. You do not need to include lots of detail. A brief overview is enough to get started.

What happens next

Once I receive your message, I’ll reply with next steps. This usually includes a short intake form to gather a little more detail about your space and the kind of support you need.

From there, we can arrange a quick call if needed and decide the right next step, including the most appropriate assessment and how best to move forward.

There is no obligation at this stage. The aim is simply to understand what support would be most helpful. All enquiries are handled with care and without judgement. If I’m not the right person to help, I will let you know honestly once I have enough information to do so.
